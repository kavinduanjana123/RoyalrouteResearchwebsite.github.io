Project Scope

Literature Survey

Tourist arrivals to Sri Lanka

In many nations, including Sri Lanka, the travel and tourism sector is a significant industry. Sri Lanka has gained popularity as a travel destination for travelers from all over the world thanks to its rich cultural history, beautiful landscapes, and tropical climate. Tourists require a well-planned itinerary that takes into consideration their preferences, interests, and budget to make their trip to Sri Lanka fun and memorable. However, organizing a trip might be difficult, especially for foreign visitors who are not familiar with the geography and culture of the area. We suggest a collaborative filtering-based tour recommendation system that makes use of user comments and contextual data like seasonal events to solve this issue. To forecast a user's interests and preferences based on their prior activity and the behavior of other like users, recommendation systems frequently utilize a method called collaborative filtering. Our technology can provide customized tour suggestions that take into consideration each visitor's specific tastes and the activities taking place during their stay by examining their preferences and comments from prior international visitors to Sri Lanka. Our system's objective is to give international visitors a useful tool for organizing their trip to Sri Lanka. According on their tastes, interests, and budget, our algorithm will suggest trips that also take into account the seasonal events taking place when they are here. Our method seeks to deliver accurate and varied tour recommendations that improve the visitor's overall experience in Sri Lanka by utilizing collaborative filtering and contextual data.

Research Problem & Solution

Proposed Problem

The tourism industry in Sri Lanka faces a number of challenges when it comes to providing efficient and secure modes of transportation for visitors. Currently, there is no mechanism in place to track and analyze industry trends, making it difficult to regulate the amount of time tourists spend on various activities or to ensure their health and safety. Additionally, tourists visiting a new place may lack familiarity with the available modes of transportation and service facilities, and hotel management may struggle to meet their demands. In light of these challenges, there is a clear need for a smart tour planning system that can help to enhance the tourist experience in Sri Lanka. The proposed system will aim to address the limitations of the current system by utilizing machine learning to recommend destinations, transportation methods, and accommodations based on the preferences of foreign tourists. The system will also consider seasonal occasions and social media feedback to provide a complete tour experience. The main objective of this research is to develop a smart tour planning system that will address the challenges faced by tourists visiting Sri Lanka and help to enhance their overall experience. By providing efficient and secure transportation options, recommending tour schedules and destinations, and suggesting accommodations based on feedback from other tourists, the proposed system has the potential to transform the way tourists plan their trips and support the growth of the tourism industry in SriLanka.


Proposed Solution

The proposed solution aims to address the challenges faced by foreign tourists when traveling in Sri Lanka. By utilizing machine learning algorithms, the "Smart Tour Planning System" will recommend the best destinations and transportation methods to the user based on their preferences. It will also schedule the tour by proposing the most efficient routes and transportation methods, taking into consideration the feedback of other foreign tourists who have visited. The system will analyze user preferences and Sri Lankan seasonal events to recommend a complete tour itinerary, and recommend the best 3 accommodation places to the user based on social media and user feedback. This system will provide a convenient and personalized travel experience for foreign tourists, allowing them to explore Sri Lanka with ease and confidence.

Research Objectives

User Preference-Based Destination and Transportation Recommendations:

The system will suggest suitable destinations and transportation methods for the user based on their preferences.

Collaborative Filtering for Complete Tour Recommendations:

The system will suggest a complete tour by analyzing user preferences and Sri Lankan seasonal events through the use of collaborative filtering.

Accommodation Recommendations Based on Social Media and User Feedback:

The system will recommend the best three accommodation places for the user by analyzing the social media accounts of the accommodations and user  feedback.

Tour Scheduling with Optimal Routes:

The system will schedule the tour by proposing the best routes using feedback from foreign tourists who have previously visited the area

Methodology

Figure 2. High Level Architecture of the system.

The process of suggesting hotels and accommodations involves collecting comprehensive data on the target area, including hotel names, ratings, prices, amenities, and user reviews. This data is then preprocessed and analyzed using machine learning algorithms. A recommendation system is implemented, considering user preferences and historical data. A ranking algorithm is used to provide personalized suggestions, considering location relevance, budget constraints, and tourist attraction distance. Real-time availability checks are integrated to ensure accommodations are available during the user's chosen travel dates. The system offers an interactive user interface for customization and feedback, with robust privacy and security measures and continuous performance evaluation for an optimal travel experience.
